About Me

Hello, my name is Ruth Agorrilla and I'm a 4th-year Mechanical Engineering undergraduate student at the University of California, Merced. Currently, my interests are in CAD design, 3D printing, and a little bit of coding, and I want to work on my own projects in order to experiment with a different medium where I can explore the ideas that I have stored up in my noggin.
I believe that engineering has the potential to be a means of creativity and can be used to create anything if one were to imagine it. The breadth of it is similar to that of being given paint tools and a blank canvas, as similar to how endless are the things you can paint on the canvas, the broadness of engineering opens doors to multiple possiblities with what you can do with it. This is the mindset that I want to go in with when I tackle projects that I'm passionate about.
