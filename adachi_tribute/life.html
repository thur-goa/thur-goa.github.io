Adachi's Life

February 1984 Tohru Adachi was born

2005 Enlisted in the police force at 21

March 2011 Got involved in the scandal between Misuzu Hiiragi, Mayumi Yamano, and Taro Namatame

April 2011 Began the series of murders in Inaba, starting with Saki, an acquaintance of Yosuke Hanamura

November 2011 Hid his involvement in the Inaba murders by pointing the blame towards Taro Namatame, resulting in Nanako Dojima's involvement and near death

December 2011 Exposed as the murderer by the Investigation Team and pursued in the Midnight Channel; is arrested after his defeat

January - March 2012 Revealed to the Investigation Team that a higher being was potentially behind the orchestration of Inaba's mystery
