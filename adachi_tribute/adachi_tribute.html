Tohru Adachi

Aka the goofy cabbage man

Adachi's Life

February 1984 Tohru Adachi was born

2005 Enlisted in the police force at 21

March 2011 Got involved in the scandal between Misuzu Hiiragi, Mayumi Yamano, and Taro Namatame

April 2011 Began the series of murders in Inaba, starting with Saki, an acquaintance of Yosuke Hanamura

November 2011 Hid his involvement in the Inaba murders by pointing the blame towards Taro Namatame, resulting in Nanako Dojima's involvement and near death

December 2011 Exposed as the murderer by the Investigation Team and pursued in the Midnight Channel; is arrested after his defeat

January - March 2012 Revealed to the Investigation Team that a higher being was potentially behind the orchestration of Inaba's mystery

"Let's be honest. There's nothing great about the real world, is there? It's just dull and annoying as hell. No one accepts that's the way things are... They're just stuck with it because they can't deny it, either. Those who actually succeed in life... They just happen to be born with the magic ticket called "talent." If you don't have it, you can either accept or deny that fact until you die. That's your only choice. Once you realize that, all you have left in life is despair. The ultimate game over. Wouldn't it be better if that kind of reality was wiped away?"

--Tohru Adachi, Persona 4

I honestly think that Tohru Adachi is an extremely well-written character in Persona 4. In a nutshell, he foils Yu Narukami, the motivation that keeps him, the Investigation Team, and his confidants moving forward, where he represents the nihilism that is associated when one has an unhealthy outlook on life. If you want to learn more about this complex character, I recommend that you play through Persona 4 (the original or Golden, your choice), or you can read about his character here.
