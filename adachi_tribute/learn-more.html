the tohru adachi page

I honestly think that Tohru Adachi is an extremely well-written character in Persona 4. In a nutshell, he foils Yu Narukami, the motivation that keeps him, the Investigation Team, and his confidants moving forward, where he represents the nihilism that is associated when one has an unhealthy outlook on life. If you want to learn more about this complex character, I recommend that you play through Persona 4 (the original or Golden, your choice), or you can read about his character here.
